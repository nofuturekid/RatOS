Voron V2.4

The Voron V2.4 is a floating gantry Core XY printer in 3 medium sizes designed by Voron. The v2.4 can be bought in kits from multiple vendors, or selfsourced by following the official sourcing guide.

Supported Boards​

  • BIGTREETECH Octopus v1.1
  • BIGTREETECH Octopus v1.1 F407
  • BIGTREETECH Octopus Pro 446
  • BIGTREETECH Octopus Pro 429
  • BIGTREETECH Octopus Pro H723
  • BIGTREETECH Manta M8P
  • Fysetc Spider v1.1

4th z driver.​

The 4th z driver needed to drive the Voron 2.4 gantry goes in the slot to the left of the "LEFT Z" driver on the board of your choice.
